## Mechanism of Action

Survodutide is a dual GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist. The glucagon receptor component promotes hepatic fat oxidation and energy expenditure, which may provide benefit in MASH beyond glycaemic and weight effects. Boehringer Ingelheim and Zealand are co-developing it in Phase 3 trials for both obesity and MASH.

## Status

Phase 3 trials ongoing for obesity (SYNCHRONIZE programme) and MASH. Results expected 2026–2027. Not FDA-approved.
