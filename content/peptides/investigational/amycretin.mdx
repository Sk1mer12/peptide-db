## Mechanism of Action

Amycretin is a first-in-class unimolecular agent that simultaneously agonises both GLP-1 and amylin receptors. By combining these two complementary mechanisms in a single molecule rather than a fixed-dose combination of two separate drugs (as in CagriSema), Novo Nordisk aims to achieve pharmacokinetic simplicity and potentially improved tolerability.

## Phase 2 Data

Phase 1 oral tablet data (published 2024) showed ~13% weight loss at 12 weeks in adults with overweight/obesity — substantially higher than what is typically seen in comparable short-term studies with oral semaglutide. Phase 2 studies are ongoing.

## Status

Early development. Not FDA-approved. No Phase 3 initiation announced as of early 2025.
