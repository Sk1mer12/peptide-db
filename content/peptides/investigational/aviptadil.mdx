## Mechanism of Action

VIP is a 28-amino acid neuropeptide that binds VPAC1 and VPAC2 receptors, producing vasodilation, bronchodilation, and potent immunomodulation (suppression of pro-inflammatory cytokines, promotion of T-regulatory cells). Type II alveolar cells express very high levels of VPAC2, making the lung a primary target organ.

## Status

Relief Therapeutics' ZYESAMI (inhaled aviptadil) did not receive FDA approval for COVID-19 ARDS. Research is ongoing in other respiratory and pulmonary hypertension indications. The compound has no approved use anywhere as of early 2025.
