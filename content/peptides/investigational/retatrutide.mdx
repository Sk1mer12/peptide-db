## Mechanism of Action

Retatrutide is a unimolecular triagonist activating GIP, GLP-1, and glucagon receptors simultaneously. The addition of glucagon receptor agonism to the dual GIP/GLP-1 mechanism of tirzepatide is hypothesised to provide additional energy expenditure by increasing basal metabolic rate and hepatic fat oxidation.

## Phase 3 Programme

The TRIUMPH programme (Phase 3) is ongoing. Phase 2 data published in NEJM (2023) showed 24.2% body weight reduction at 48 weeks in the highest-dose group — surpassing all previously reported results for any pharmacological agent. Phase 3 results are expected in 2025–2026.

## Status

Not FDA-approved. Not available outside of clinical trials. Phase 3 NDA submission is anticipated pending trial completion.
