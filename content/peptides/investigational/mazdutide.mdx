## Mechanism of Action

Mazdutide is a GLP-1/glucagon receptor dual agonist based on an oxyntomodulin scaffold. Oxyntomodulin is an endogenous peptide secreted from L cells that naturally acts on both GLP-1R and GCGR. Mazdutide is optimised for once-weekly dosing via a fatty acid acylation strategy.

## Status

Phase 3 programme ongoing in China. NMPA submission expected. Not approved or available in the US, EU, or most global markets.
