## Mechanism of Action

Pemvidutide is a GLP-1/glucagon receptor dual agonist designed with a balanced receptor activity ratio intended to favour fat mass loss over lean mass loss. Phase 2 MOMENTUM trial data showed ~15% body weight loss with apparent preservation of lean mass — a differentiated feature compared to GLP-1-only agents.

## Status

Phase 2 complete. Phase 2b/3 planning announced. Not FDA-approved.
