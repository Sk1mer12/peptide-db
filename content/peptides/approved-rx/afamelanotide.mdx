## Background

Afamelanotide (Melanotan I) is a linear α-MSH analogue with [Nle4, D-Phe7] substitutions that enhance potency and metabolic stability. Unlike Melanotan II, it was refined for MC1R selectivity to stimulate melanin production without the non-specific MC3R/MC4R effects (sexual arousal, appetite). It is approved as Scenesse for EPP — a rare genetic condition causing intense pain upon light exposure.

## Distinction from Melanotan II

Afamelanotide and Melanotan II share the same original research lineage but are distinct compounds with different receptor selectivity profiles, safety data, and legal status. Afamelanotide is FDA-approved for EPP; Melanotan II is not approved for any use.
