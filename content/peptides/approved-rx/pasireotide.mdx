## Mechanism of Action

Pasireotide binds somatostatin receptors 1, 2, 3, and 5 with high affinity, with particularly strong binding to SSTR5, which is overexpressed in corticotroph adenomas causing Cushing's disease. This multi-receptor profile enables suppression of ACTH secretion from corticotroph tumours.

## Clinical Indications

- **Cushing's disease**: for adults not amenable to surgery or where surgery failed
- **Acromegaly**: in patients inadequately controlled with other somatostatin analogues
