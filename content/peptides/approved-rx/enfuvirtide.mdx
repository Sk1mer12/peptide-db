## Mechanism of Action

Enfuvirtide is a 36-amino acid synthetic peptide derived from the HR2 heptad repeat region of HIV-1 gp41. It binds to the HR1 region of gp41 during the viral fusion process, blocking the conformational change required for membrane fusion and viral entry into CD4+ T cells.

## Clinical Indications

- **HIV-1 infection**: salvage therapy in treatment-experienced adults and children (≥6 years) with evidence of replication despite antiretroviral therapy
