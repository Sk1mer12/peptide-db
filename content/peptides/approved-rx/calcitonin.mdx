## Mechanism of Action

Calcitonin salmon binds calcitonin receptors on osteoclasts, inhibiting bone resorption. It also has analgesic effects (possibly via endorphin release) that are exploited in vertebral fracture pain management.

## Clinical Indications

- **Postmenopausal osteoporosis** (no longer first-line)
- **Paget's disease of bone**
- **Hypercalcaemia of malignancy**
- **Acute vertebral fracture pain** (off-label analgesic use)
