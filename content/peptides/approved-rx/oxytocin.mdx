## Mechanism of Action

Oxytocin binds to oxytocin receptors (OXTRs), G-protein-coupled receptors expressed in uterine myometrium, mammary gland, and the central nervous system. Uterine OXTR expression increases dramatically near term, mediating smooth muscle contraction and uterine involution postpartum.

## Clinical Indications

- **Labour induction and augmentation**
- **Postpartum haemorrhage prevention and treatment**
- **Lactation assistance** (mammary milk ejection)
