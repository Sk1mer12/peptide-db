## Mechanism of Action

Cetrorelix is a synthetic decapeptide antagonist that competitively binds GnRH receptors in the pituitary, producing immediate suppression of LH (and to a lesser extent FSH) secretion without the initial agonist flare seen with GnRH agonists. This makes it ideal for preventing premature LH surges during controlled ovarian stimulation.

## Clinical Indications

- **Controlled ovarian stimulation**: prevention of premature LH surges in women undergoing assisted reproduction
