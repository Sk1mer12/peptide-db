## Mechanism of Action

Zilucoplan is a synthetic macrocyclic peptide that binds complement protein C5 and inhibits its cleavage into C5a and C5b. This prevents formation of the membrane attack complex (MAC) that destroys neuromuscular junction components in myasthenia gravis. It is self-administered subcutaneously once daily, offering a meaningful convenience advantage over IV eculizumab.

## Clinical Indications

- **Generalised myasthenia gravis (gMG)**: anti-AChR antibody-positive adults with Class II–IV disease
