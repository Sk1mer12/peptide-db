## Mechanism of Action

Teduglutide is a GLP-2 receptor agonist (with Gly2 substitution for DPP-IV resistance). GLP-2 receptor activation in the small intestinal epithelium promotes crypt cell proliferation, villus growth, and intestinal blood flow, enhancing mucosal surface area and nutrient/fluid absorption.

## Clinical Indications

- **Short bowel syndrome (SBS)** dependent on parenteral nutrition: reduces parenteral support volume
