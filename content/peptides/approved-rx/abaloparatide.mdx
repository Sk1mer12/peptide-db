## Mechanism of Action

Abaloparatide is a synthetic 34-amino acid analogue of human PTHrP (parathyroid hormone-related protein). It selectively activates the PTH1R receptor in its RG conformation, which is linked to bone anabolic signalling, with shorter exposure to resorptive pathways compared to teriparatide. This theoretical selectivity may translate to a more favourable bone formation-to-resorption ratio.

## Clinical Indications

- **Postmenopausal osteoporosis** at high fracture risk: anabolic therapy for up to 2 years
