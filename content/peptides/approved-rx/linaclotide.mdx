## Mechanism of Action

Linaclotide is a 14-amino acid peptide that acts as an agonist at guanylate cyclase-C (GC-C) receptors on intestinal epithelial cells. Activation increases intracellular cGMP, which opens CFTR chloride channels, driving fluid secretion into the intestinal lumen. The increased cGMP also activates afferent sensory neurons, reducing visceral pain signals.

## Clinical Indications

- **IBS-C**: symptom relief including abdominal pain and constipation
- **Chronic idiopathic constipation (CIC)**
