## Mechanism of Action

Octreotide is an octapeptide analogue of somatostatin that binds to somatostatin receptors (primarily SSTR2 and SSTR5), suppressing secretion of GH, IGF-1, insulin, glucagon, and various gastrointestinal hormones.

## Clinical Indications

- **Acromegaly**: GH and IGF-1 suppression
- **Carcinoid syndrome**: symptom control (flushing, diarrhoea)
- **VIPomas / GRFomas**: symptom management
- **Variceal bleeding**: acute adjunct therapy
