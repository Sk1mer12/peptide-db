## Mechanism of Action

Triptorelin is a decapeptide GnRH agonist. Continuous stimulation of pituitary GnRH receptors leads to receptor downregulation, with subsequent suppression of LH, FSH, and gonadal sex hormones to castrate levels after 2–4 weeks.

## Clinical Indications

- **Advanced prostate cancer**: androgen deprivation
- **Central precocious puberty**
- **Endometriosis**
- **Uterine fibroids**
