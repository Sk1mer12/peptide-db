## Mechanism of Action

Leuprolide is a synthetic GnRH (LHRH) analogue with ~100× greater potency than endogenous GnRH. Continuous administration desensitises pituitary GnRH receptors, leading to a paradoxical reduction in LH and FSH secretion and consequently suppressed gonadal testosterone/oestrogen production after an initial flare.

## Clinical Indications

- **Advanced prostate cancer**: androgen deprivation therapy
- **Endometriosis**: pain management and lesion reduction
- **Uterine fibroids**: preoperative reduction
- **Precocious puberty**: hypothalamic amenorrhoea
