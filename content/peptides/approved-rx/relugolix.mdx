## Notes

Relugolix is a small molecule GnRH antagonist, not a peptide. It is listed here because it is part of the GnRH antagonist therapeutic class and is frequently compared with peptide-based agents in the same space. As an oral once-daily tablet, it offers a significant convenience advantage and avoids injection site reactions associated with peptide-based GnRH antagonists like degarelix.

## Clinical Indications

- **Advanced prostate cancer**: androgen deprivation (superior cardiovascular safety signal vs. leuprolide in HERO trial)
