## Mechanism of Action

Teriparatide represents the first 34 amino acids of human PTH. Intermittent administration (once-daily subcutaneous) activates PTH1R on osteoblasts, stimulating bone formation preferentially over resorption — an anabolic mechanism distinct from antiresorptive bisphosphonates.

## Clinical Indications

- **Postmenopausal osteoporosis** at high fracture risk
- **Male osteoporosis**
- **Glucocorticoid-induced osteoporosis**

## Key Clinical Data

The pivotal FPT trial demonstrated 65–69% reductions in vertebral fracture risk vs. placebo. Treatment is limited to a cumulative lifetime exposure of 2 years.
