## Mechanism of Action

Bremelanotide activates melanocortin receptors, particularly MC3R and MC4R, in the central nervous system. MC4R activation in the hypothalamus is thought to modulate sexual arousal pathways. Unlike PDE5 inhibitors, it works centrally rather than on peripheral vascular tissue.

## Clinical Indications

- **HSDD in premenopausal women**: taken as needed ~45 minutes before sexual activity

## Notes

Bremelanotide (as PT-141) is widely sold in the grey market for libido enhancement in both sexes. The FDA-approved version (Vyleesi) is indicated only for women with HSDD. Grey-market PT-141 is of uncertain purity and is not approved for human use.
