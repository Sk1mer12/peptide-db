## Mechanism of Action

Degarelix is a 10-amino acid GnRH antagonist that competitively and reversibly binds GnRH receptors, producing immediate and sustained suppression of LH, FSH, and testosterone without the testosterone flare observed with GnRH agonists. This makes it preferable in patients with vertebral metastases or severe obstructive symptoms where a testosterone flare is dangerous.

## Clinical Indications

- **Advanced hormone-sensitive prostate cancer**: androgen deprivation therapy
