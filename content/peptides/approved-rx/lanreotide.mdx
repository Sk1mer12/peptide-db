## Mechanism of Action

Lanreotide is a cyclic octapeptide somatostatin analogue with high affinity for SSTR2 and SSTR5. Its prolonged half-life arises from the depot formulation, which forms a liquid crystal structure at physiological pH, enabling slow sustained release from the injection site.

## Clinical Indications

- **Acromegaly**: GH/IGF-1 normalisation
- **Gastroenteropancreatic neuroendocrine tumours (GEP-NETs)**: tumour growth control (CLARINET trial data)
