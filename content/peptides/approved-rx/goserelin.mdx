## Mechanism of Action

Goserelin is a synthetic decapeptide analogue of GnRH. Like leuprolide, it continuously stimulates and then desensitises GnRH receptors in the pituitary, leading to castrate levels of sex steroids after an initial flare.

## Clinical Indications

- **Prostate cancer**: androgen deprivation (locally advanced and metastatic)
- **Breast cancer**: hormonal manipulation in premenopausal women
- **Endometriosis**: pain and lesion management
- **Assisted reproduction**: ovarian suppression in IVF protocols
