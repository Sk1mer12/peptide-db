## Mechanism of Action

Ganirelix is a synthetic decapeptide GnRH antagonist that competitively blocks pituitary GnRH receptors, immediately suppressing LH (and FSH) without an initial agonist flare. This makes it ideal for preventing premature LH surges during controlled ovarian stimulation without the 2–3 week pituitary downregulation required with GnRH agonists.

## Clinical Indications

- **Controlled ovarian stimulation for IVF/ICSI**: prevention of premature LH surges in women undergoing ART
