## Background

KPV is the C-terminal tripeptide fragment of α-MSH that retains its anti-inflammatory activity without the full melanocortin receptor binding profile of the parent peptide. Preclinical research suggests it can reduce gut inflammation in rodent models of IBD (colitis) via MC1R-dependent and independent pathways. It has shown activity when delivered orally in nanoparticle formulations in mice.

## Status

FDA placed KPV on its Category 2 list for compounding. No clinical trials of KPV in humans have been registered or completed.
