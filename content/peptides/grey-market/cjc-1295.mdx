## Background

CJC-1295 is a GHRH analogue based on the GRF(1-29) sequence with four amino acid substitutions to improve protease resistance. The "DAC" variant incorporates a maleimide group that covalently binds to serum albumin, dramatically extending its half-life to ~8 days.

## Regulatory Position

FDA placed GHRH analogues (including CJC-1295 and sermorelin) under scrutiny. CJC-1295 specifically is not on any approved drug list globally. The combination of CJC-1295 with ipamorelin is frequently marketed in grey-market contexts as a "GH peptide stack."
