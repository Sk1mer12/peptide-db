## Background

Mechano Growth Factor (MGF) is an alternative splicing variant of IGF-1 (IGF-1Ec) that is produced locally in mechanically stimulated muscle and acts on satellite cells (muscle stem cells) to promote repair. PEG-MGF uses polyethylene glycol conjugation to extend its half-life from minutes to days. Animal studies report enhanced muscle recovery after injury. No human pharmacology or safety data has been published.
