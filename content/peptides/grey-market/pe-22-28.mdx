## Background

PE-22-28 is derived from research on spadin, a peptide fragment of propeptide derived from NTSR3/sortilin processing. Spadin was shown to block TREK-1 two-pore-domain potassium channels and produce rapid antidepressant effects in mice. PE-22-28 is a synthetic analogue reported to have improved pharmacological properties vs. spadin. All data is from one French research group using rodent models. No human studies exist.
