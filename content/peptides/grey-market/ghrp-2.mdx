## Background

GHRP-2 (pralmorelin) is a D-Ala–D-βNal–Ala–Trp–D-Phe–Lys–NH2 hexapeptide that activates ghrelin receptors, stimulating pulsatile GH release. Unlike ipamorelin, it also stimulates cortisol and prolactin, which is considered a disadvantage for bodybuilding use. It is approved in Japan as a diagnostic agent for GH deficiency testing, but not for therapeutic use.

## Status

Not FDA-approved. WADA-prohibited. Available only through grey-market research chemical vendors.
