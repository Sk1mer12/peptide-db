## Background

Pinealon is a synthetic tripeptide developed by the same group that produced Epitalon (Khavinson's group, St. Petersburg Institute of Bioregulation). It is proposed to cross the blood-brain barrier and regulate gene expression in neuronal cells, potentially reducing oxidative stress and protecting against neurodegeneration. Like Epitalon and other peptides from this research group, the evidence base is limited to Russian-language preclinical publications with no independent replication.
