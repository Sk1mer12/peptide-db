## Background

GHRP-6 was one of the first synthetic peptides developed specifically as a GH secretagogue. Its potent appetite-stimulating effects (mediated via ghrelin receptor activation in the hypothalamus and vagal afferents) made it less favoured for GH-release-only purposes compared to later compounds like ipamorelin, but this effect is sometimes sought deliberately in recovery contexts.

## Status

Not approved anywhere for therapeutic use. WADA-prohibited as a GH secretagogue.
