## Background

5-Amino-1MQ is technically a small molecule (quaternary amine), not a peptide. It inhibits NNMT, which methylates and inactivates nicotinamide — a key NAD+ precursor. By inhibiting NNMT, 5-Amino-1MQ theoretically increases intracellular NAD+ levels and activates sirtuin pathways. Mouse studies showed reduced adiposity and improved metabolic markers. The research base is very limited and primarily from one group.

## Status

No human trials. No IND. Available from a small number of research chemical vendors with minimal characterisation of purity or stability.
